The Custom Champion Game Plan

1

The Challenger's Briefing

We start with an in-depth strategy session to understand your unique energy challenge, arena constraints, and operational goals. Our engineers work with you to define the project scope, timeline, and budget.

  • Detailed energy load analysis
  • Site assessment (on-site or remote)
  • Review of technical and environmental challenges
  • Budget planning and cost optimization
  • Timeline establishment
2

Engineering the Blueprint for Victory

Our corner team of engineers designs a custom solar champion. We provide detailed system diagrams, performance simulations, and equipment specifications for your review and feedback.

  • System modeling and energy simulation
  • Component selection and compatibility analysis
  • Structural and electrical engineering plans
  • Financial modeling (ROI, LCOE)
  • Design refinement iterations
3

The Sparring Session

For truly unique contenders, we develop and test prototypes of key components to validate performance and ensure the final design is ready for the main event.

  • Component-level bench testing
  • Small-scale pilot system deployment
  • Performance data collection and analysis
  • Final design validation and approval
  • Final design approval process
4

The Championship Build

Once the design is approved, our certified technicians begin the build. We maintain strict quality control at every stage, with regular progress updates to keep you in the loop.

  • Procurement of specialized components
  • Professional installation and site management
  • Continuous quality assurance checks
  • System commissioning and final inspection
  • Grid interconnection and permitting
5

The Title Defense

After commissioning, we hand over the system and provide comprehensive training. Our post-installation support ensures your champion's optimal performance for years to come.

  • System handover and documentation
  • Operator training and best practices
  • 24/7 remote monitoring setup
  • Scheduled maintenance plans
  • Ongoing customer support and reorders
